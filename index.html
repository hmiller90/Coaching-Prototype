Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the coach matching process work?
After you complete your purchase, our Coaching Concierge team reviews your profile, selected track, and customization notes to identify the best-fit coach from our network. You'll receive an email introduction to your coach within 48 hours — often sooner.
Can I switch coaches if it's not a good fit?
Absolutely. We want you to feel confident in your match. If after your intro call you'd prefer a different coach, simply reach out to our team at coaching@wallstreetprep.com and we'll work to find a better fit at no additional cost.
How do I access the online learning portal?
After your purchase, you'll receive an email with account setup instructions. Once logged in, you'll have access to curated learning journeys and bonus courses relevant to your coaching track.
What's the difference between the coaching packages?
The packages differ in total hours and price per hour. Quick Support (1–3 hrs at $400/hr) is best for targeted, one-off questions. Skill Building (4–9 hrs at $350/hr) is ideal for focused skill development. Performance Accelerator (10–19 hrs at $340/hr) is for more intensive preparation. The Mastery Track (20+ hrs at $330/hr) provides the deepest engagement.
Are sessions conducted live or asynchronously?
Most coaching is conducted live via video call (Zoom or similar platforms). The Resume & Mock Interview track also includes asynchronous email-based feedback.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a satisfaction guarantee on unused hours. If you've completed your intro call but haven't yet started your first full session, you may request a full refund on unused hours. After coaching begins, refunds are prorated based on unused hours.
How experienced are WSP coaches?
All Wall Street Prep coaches are vetted practitioners with real-world experience at leading firms including bulge bracket investment banks, mega-fund private equity firms, and top hedge funds.
How long does it take to be paired with a coach?
You can typically expect a coach introduction within 24–48 hours after purchase, with most matches completed in under 24 hours.
Can I be matched with a coach faster than 24 hours? What's the fastest turnaround?
During checkout, you'll be asked how quickly you'd like to begin — select the "urgent" option if you need expedited matching.

In many cases, we can introduce you to a coach in as little as 4 hours. For same-day matching, requests must be submitted by 3:00 PM ET on business days. While we do our best to accommodate urgent requests, same-day matching is not guaranteed and depends on coach availability.
